Clean Water and Sanitation

Why SDG 6?

Our "Save Every Drop" water tracker project directly aligns with Sustainable Development Goal 6: Clean Water and Sanitation. This goal aims to ensure availability and sustainable management of water and sanitation for all by 2030. With global water scarcity affecting over 2 billion people and projected to worsen due to climate change and population growth, household-level water conservation has become critical.

By empowering families to track their daily water consumption across activities like showering, laundry, dishwashing, and outdoor watering, our website raises awareness about individual water footprints and encourages behavioral change toward more responsible usage patterns.

Specific Target: 6.4

"By 2030, substantially increase water-use efficiency across all sectors and ensure sustainable withdrawals and supply of freshwater to address water scarcity and substantially reduce the number of people suffering from water scarcity."

Our tracker directly supports this target by helping households measure and reduce their water consumption, thereby increasing water-use efficiency at the grassroots level. Small changes in millions of homes can collectively make a massive difference in preserving freshwater resources.
